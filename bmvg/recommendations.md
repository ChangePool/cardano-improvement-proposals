# Participation in Beyond Minimum Viable Governance Workshops

In April 2026, the following recommendations are captured on the [Beyond MVG](https://beyond-mvg.vercel.app/) Web site for submission to Input Output.

## _ADA Holder Metrics_: Ensure People Do What They Do Best

Currently, in the Cardano ecosystem how much someone is listened to seems to be reduced to how much ADA someone holds (the more the better) and how much someone is a developer (the more technical, the better).

Experts should be taken seriously in their respective fields throughout the Cardano ecosystem. Use validated digital IDs and perhaps zero-knowledge proofs offered using Midnight throughout the Cardano community so that individual skills and knowledge, including professional expertise outside Cardano, may be recognized and acknowledged.

Cardano needs to hear the voices of teachers, accountants, lawyers, engineers, academics, trades, politicians, business people, psychologists and others for example offering what they do and know best in addition to the considerations of developers.

People who are professionals in the real world who are ignored, belittled, disrespected, ridiculed or banned in the Cardano ecosystem for sharing their expertise rightly will and do leave, not return, and tell everyone they know not to bother with Cardano.

## _DRep Metrics_: Cardano Branch Offices

Pilot Cardano brick and mortar offices (or equivalent online resources) staffed by DReps where people may go to seek financial services supporting their day-to-day banking needs as well as their long-term hopes and dreams in life, similar to how fiat banks maintain branches in the community for customers. However, instead of funding the future based on debt, serve customers by putting people in touch with like-minded successful projects in the Cardano community. In Cardano branch offices, the primary capital is social.

For example, if someone wants to fund a library for the first time to serve or give back to a small community where they grew up, DReps in the local Cardano branch may help connect the project with competent developers to help set up a stake pool, and then existing community members who are known to hold consistent values may be informed about the stake pool as a potential delegation in order to help create a better world using Cardano. Rewards that the stake pool receives funds the intended project. DReps help manage such projects successfully to completion.

Shift the current narrative that “delegators should pinch pennies to support the largest stake pools and gain the most rewards” to “all investments in the Cardano ecosystem receive essentially the same, fair rate of return, so invest in ways that reflect your values and beliefs.” If the latter statement is not true, then Cardano will not survive because the protocol would be uncooperative and exclusive rather than inclusive. Web3 projects by definition depend on cooperation for success more than the traditional financial system does.

## _SPO Metrics_: Securing the Network is a Means, Not An End

Shift the narrative that the primary goal of stake pool operation is “securing the network” to “stake pools are banks intended and expected to drive healthy economy, transaction and business in society, with network security occurring as a natural by-product.” For example, form a task force of DReps to help Cardano explorers relying on gambling ads for revenue shift to a fee model based on stake pool rewards. In other words, help each explorer currently displaying gambling ads develop a healthy stake pool supported by sufficient delegators in the community, including delegations from founding entities as may be needed, in exchange for the removal of gambling ads. Put out messaging that Cardano wants and needs the community to support these stake pools for the benefit of Cardano as a whole. Demonstrate by example to the community how stakes pools are designed to facilitate cooperation to achieve actions to benefit the commons as well as—not just—the self individual delegator and stake pool operator.

A Cardano ecosystem without explorers relying on gambling ads is family friendly and primes Cardano for long-term, massive growth into future generations. For example, the same way that parents may start saving for a child’s education in fiat when the child is born, we want parents to understand that saving for a child’s future on Cardano in ADA is a sound investment. Such investments also encourage today’s children to become tomorrow’s users of Cardano. Update the constitution to reflect such human values of cooperation and service in addition to self interest, which is also important and necessary.

## _Constitutional Committee Metrics_: Champion a Cause

Over the longer term, the Cardano ecosystem may benefit from championing one or more causes based on human values, beyond the numeric values on which the Cardano blockchain depends technically. Stop promoting Cardano as “the best blockchain for everyone, for everything,” which lacks focus and appears desperate. Taking an industry perspective, Cardano having recently spent more than one year focussed full time on developing and implementing cooperative governance technology and processes makes Cardano “the governance chain.” Therefore, Cardano needs to demonstrate how governance improves with Cardano by using successfully in house the governance products and services that Cardano now offers.

Governance was a problem that no one asked Cardano to solve. However, here we are now.

Prepare a CIP asking the community to vote for causes to which Cardano may contribute positively in the world. For example, position Cardano as an economic framework supporting peace in the world. With so much war expanding currently, the world is facing a future in which numerous societies will be rebuilding infrastructure and processes, including governance. Seek to make a genuine contribution to the [Board of Peace](https://boardofpeace.org/) established in January 2026. Prepare a delegation to seek an audience with the Pope to explain how Cardano is a technology to help foster cooperative economic development and governance globally. Ask the Pope how he believes that Cardano may best play a role in international efforts to stop the currently escalating wars.

## _General Governance_: Accommodate Change

In terms of scaling Cardano for mass adoption, everyone does not need to use the same governance model. Allow for and expect the organic emergence of complementary or alternative governance models and processes from the community and the world embracing Cardano, in addition to the CIP-1694 based model implemented today, similar to how a RUST-based alternative Cardano Node software currently is under development.

A key indicator of the maturity or sustainability of Cardano in the hands of community governance will be a community voting to share power—such as giving some power back to the founding entities—rather than constantly seeking to claim as much power as possible for itself.
